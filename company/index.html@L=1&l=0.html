      SPECIAL TRANSFORMERS

      Since 1980 we have been developing and manufacturing transformers.
      As specialists right from day one for the control of high capacities for
      medium and high frequencies, we are the point of contact for renowned
      industrial customers and research institutions.

       

      We support our customers from the early stages of project planning of
      their facilities and installations, and we provide solutions for demanding
      tasks worldwide. Over the years we have grown substantially and we
      look forward to continue this success story.
